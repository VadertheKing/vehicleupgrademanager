AUTOMOBILE UPGRADES REPORT

Based on the previously recommended automobile upgrades, the following trends can be identified:

ENGINE UPGRADES:
- Replacing the air filter.
- Upgrading the cold air intake system.
- Installing a turbocharger.

SUSPENSION UPGRADES:
- Upgrading the shock absorbers.
- Installing a lowering kit.
- Installing coilovers.

EXHAUST SYSTEM UPGRADES:
- Replacing the muffler.
- Installing a cat-back exhaust system.
- Installing a full headers and exhaust system.
